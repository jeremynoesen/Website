I'm Jeremy

I am a graduate student in Computer Engineering at Iowa State University with an interest in embedded software.

Discovering My Interests

As a child, I was obsessed with my father’s electrical components in his garage. I'd always try to make circuits with these, with growing complexity over time.

Eventually, I was introduced to microcontrollers, and then programming. Later, I began to create Minecraft plugins, which made me love programming.

Present Day

I have explored beyond Minecraft plugins, and have dabbled in Discord bots and web design. While these were fun, they never really stuck with me.

Thanks to my experience at Iowa State University, I have discovered a strong interest in embedded software, especially in relation to smart home solutions.
